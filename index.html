The Egyptian Clothing Bank

The Egyptian Clothing Bank Foundation pledges to mitigate the effects of climate change, as the Clothing Bank Al-Masry implemented and widely disseminated its expertise in collecting and recycling clothing and textile waste in Egypt Egypt.

Through the implementation of our extensive and widely deployed programmes, expertise and technologies, which include: The necessary steps for the clothing recycling process include collection, sorting, processing, and... Then provide the following environmental benefits:

  1. Reducing the burial of clothing and textile waste, taking into account that fiber products Synthetic fibers do not decompose and natural fibers release greenhouse gases.
  2. Avoid using raw materials.
  3. Reduce energy and water consumption and avoid pollution.

Purpose:

The Egyptian Clothing Bank aims to identify the problems facing the traditional textile industry in Egypt And address these issues in order to contribute to the sustainability of the craft heritage and ensure continuous transmission The skills and knowledge of the textile industry are passed on from generation to generation.
